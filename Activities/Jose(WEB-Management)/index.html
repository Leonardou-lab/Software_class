King

∞ — Sovereign

Moves one square in any direction. The most vital piece on the board — its capture ends the game. Weak in the opening, powerful in the endgame as an attacking force.

♕

Queen

9 points

Moves any number of squares in any direction — diagonally, horizontally, or vertically. The most powerful piece; commands the board with unmatched versatility.

♖

Rook

5 points

Moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically. Towers of the battlefield; devastating in open files and lethal when doubled on the seventh rank.

♗

Bishop

3 points

Moves any number of squares diagonally, forever bound to one colour. The bishop pair controls sweeping diagonals and becomes formidable in open positions.

♘

Knight

3 points

Leaps in an L-shape: two squares in one direction then one square perpendicular. The only piece that jumps over others — prized in closed, tangled positions.

♙

Pawn

1 point

Advances one square forward; captures diagonally. Humble yet transformative — a pawn that reaches the eighth rank may be promoted to any piece, changing the tide of war.
